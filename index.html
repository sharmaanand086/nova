About Exiteco

Exiteco was formed in 2014 and is a rapidly emerging mineral processing company with a `100% owned Romanian based metal portfolio. Exiteco is not involved in mineral exploration, but rather, the processing of stockpiles acquired from local authorities in different locations across the country, our operation is based on processing, testing, logistics and export.

The main outputs produced by our processing plants are copper with gold and silver by-products at total cash costs and all-in sustaining costs well below the industry average.

Exiteco is the owner and titleholder of all its mineral resources and stockpiles throughout the country including the Baia Mare and Oradea regions of Romania. We also own and operate all aspects of production including crushing, screening, processing, material handling, production and export of bulk copper, silver &, gold pyrites commodities.

With a simplified balance sheet and an experienced management and Board of Directors, the Company has a solid platform to continue to execute its growth strategy, both organically and through value-enhancing accretive acquisitions.

Our Vision

Having secured exclusive rights to various resources across Romania, Exiteco and its related companies endeavour to become a leading secondary copper concentrate producer while maintaining its competitive advantage of cost efficiency which is well below the industry standard. Through acquisitions and expansions, we will continue our growth trajectory.
